Education

Government High School Miripora

I began my academic journey at Government High School Miripora, where I developed a strong foundation in various subjects and cultivated a passion for learning. This phase of my education was crucial in shaping my future academic and professional pursuits.

Higher Secondary Magam

After completing my schooling, I attended Higher Secondary Magam for my higher secondary education. Here, I furthered my knowledge and skills, particularly in science and mathematics, which paved the way for my interest in engineering and technology.

BE in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from NMIT

My higher education took me to NMIT, where I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). During my time at NMIT, I gained in-depth knowledge of computer science principles, software development, and engineering practices. This academic experience was instrumental in preparing me for a career in software engineering.

Passion for Computer Science

My passion for computer science has driven me to constantly explore new technologies and stay updated with the latest advancements in the field. I enjoy solving complex problems and creating efficient solutions that have a meaningful impact. This passion has been a guiding force in my academic and professional journey, motivating me to excel in every project I undertake.
